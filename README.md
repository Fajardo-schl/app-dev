# My Favorite Movie
  ![Ready Player One Poster](https://cdna.artstation.com/p/assets/images/images/027/058/104/medium/eddie-holly-ready-player-one-sm.jpg?1590481444)
**Ready Player One** is an exceptional fiction movie that was released in 2018, which is 7 years ago. Despite this, the movie showed great *artistic animations and CGI*, which captured the essence of video games and their beauty. The movie summary relates to the setting of a dystopian era where the environment looked horribly polluted and people had chaotic lifestyles. This made them want to have an escape from reality, which the VR game **OASIS** gave them. It follows the story of how a group of gamers struggled in a worldwide competition to take control of the game, which involved many challenges, both in the game and even real life. In the end, they were successful in managing to take ownership of the game before a giant corporate company takes control and kills the fun of the game and its user base. Other than the story, the biggest asset of the movie was its *breathtaking visuals and graphics* that imitated games perfectly, from crazy abilities to the surprising appearance of famous game series avatars like master chief from Halo or the giant Gundam Robot from the Gundam Series. This is why it felt *nostalgic and exciting* when I watched the movie, because I loved playing games ever since I was little, and this helped me connect with and understand the movie.
